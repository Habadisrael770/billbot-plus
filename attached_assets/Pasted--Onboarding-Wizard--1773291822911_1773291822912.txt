### 🚀 BILLBOT Feature Request: Mandatory Onboarding Wizard & Business Profile Settings

Please implement a mandatory onboarding flow (Popup/Modal Wizard) for new users immediately after their first login. Users **must not** be able to access the main Dashboard until this wizard is completed. Once completed, all these fields must be editable in the "Personal Area" (Settings -> Business Profile).

#### **1. Database Updates (User / Business Entity Model)**

Add the following fields to the database to store the business profile:

* `onboarding_completed` (Boolean, default: false)
* `business_tax_id` (String - e.g., "302347477")
* `business_names` (Array of Strings)
* `expense_categories` (Array of Strings - defaults: Office, Travel, Phone, Electricity, Internet, Insurance, Maintenance, Other)
* `business_type` (String - e.g., "Ltd. Company")
* `industry` (String - e.g., "Technology")
* `home_office_usage_percent` (Number 0-100)
* `vehicle_business_usage_percent` (Number 0-100)
* `estimated_annual_revenue` (String/Number)
* `is_vat_registered` (Boolean)
* `has_employees` (Boolean)

#### **2. UI Component: Mandatory Onboarding Wizard (Multi-step Popup)**

Create a modal that cannot be closed (no 'X' button or clicking outside) until the steps are completed.

**Step 1: Business Identification**

* **Guiding Text:** *"Let's identify your business! Add your business names and Tax IDs exactly as they appear on your documents. This helps BILLBOT automatically distinguish between invoices you issued and receipts you received."*
* **Fields:**
* Input: Tax ID / Business Number (ח.פ / ע.מ / ת.ז) + "Add" button.
* Input: Business Name on Invoices + "Add" button.



**Step 2: Expense Categorization**

* **Guiding Text:** *"How should we categorize your expenses? Select the basic categories that fit your business, or add custom ones. We'll use these to organize your expenses automatically."*
* **Fields:**
* Checkbox toggles for Base Categories: Office, Travel, Phone, Electricity, Internet, Insurance, Maintenance, Other.
* Input: Custom Categories + "Add" button.



**Step 3: Business Profile & Tax Settings**

* **Guiding Text:** *"Tell us a bit about your business. This information helps us better understand your activity and improve your expense classifications and tax calculations."*
* **Fields:**
* Dropdown: Business Type (e.g., Ltd. Company).
* Dropdown/Input: Industry (e.g., Technology).
* Number Input: Home to Office Usage % *(Helper text: If an expense is split between home and business, what is the business %?)*.
* Number Input: Vehicle Business Usage % *(Helper text: If your vehicle is used for both private and business purposes).*
* Input: Estimated Annual Revenue.
* Checkbox/Toggle: Registered for VAT.
* Checkbox/Toggle: Has Employees.



**Step 4: Completion**

* **Action:** "Save & Go to Dashboard" button.
* **Logic:** Update `onboarding_completed` to `true` in the database, close the modal, and load the main Dashboard.

#### **3. UI Component: Personal Area / Settings**

* Create a "Business Profile" tab inside the Settings menu.
* Populate this page with all the fields from the Onboarding Wizard so the user can view and edit their Tax ID, percentages, categories, and business type at any time.
